Improving Uyghur Speech Synthesis with Monolingual Transfer Learning: A Comparison of English and Russian Pretraining

This webpage serves as the companion site for the thesis Improving Uyghur Speech Synthesis with Monolingual Transfer Learning: A Comparison of English and Russian Pretraining.

The study focuses on the strategy selection problem in low-resource speech synthesis. It compares two monolingual transfer paths‚Äîusing English (LJSpeech) and Russian (CSS10-Russian) as source languages‚Äîto investigate whether structural similarity between the source and target languages significantly influences the quality of speech synthesis via transfer learning. The target language is Uyghur, and the model architecture used is FastSpeech 2.
